SoftMaker Office Standard 2016 for Linux

In addition to free-of-charge SoftMaker FreeOffice, there is also a commercial edition, SoftMaker Office Standard 2016, which adds important features:

  • Spell checking using high-quality commercial dictionaries covering 20 languages

  • Synonym dictionaries (thesauri) in eight languages

  • It not only opens the modern Microsoft formats DOCX, XLSX and PPTX seamlessly, but also saves documents in these formats.

  • Documents are presented in tabs, just like in a web browser.

  • The extended sidebar gives you a bird's eye view over your document and its stylesheets.

  • Add-ons developed by SoftMaker for the popular e-mail client Thunderbird that make working with your e-mails, tasks and calendars more convenient than ever

  • TextMaker 2016 offers many additional features, such as a stylesheet manager, envelope and label printing, extended PDF and EPUB export, mail-merging, cross references and figure captions.

  • PlanMaker 2016 offers powerful pivot tables, formula auditing, a watch window, data consolidation and scenarios.

  • Presentations 2016 lets you insert summary slides and photo albums, and is even easier to use thanks to its improved sidebar.

  • Free technical support by SoftMaker

  • ... and much more!

You can find a full list of all additional features in the document Office comparison.tmd in the folder SoftMaker below your home folder.

SoftMaker Office Professional 2016 for Linux

SoftMaker Office Professional 2016 expands upon the features of the standard edition by adding Berlitz dictionaries for translations from/to English, German, French, Italian and Spanish.
